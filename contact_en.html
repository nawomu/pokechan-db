When contacting us, please include

  • Data correction reports: It helps to specify the item (e.g., "the PP of move XX"), the current displayed value, and the correct value
  • Bug reports: Please share the page where it occurred, the steps to reproduce, your browser, and OS
  • Feature requests: Please describe the use case and how you would like to use it

About replies

As this is a personally-operated site, replies may take some time.
Please note that we may not be able to reply to every inquiry. In particular, replies to "thank-you" and "feedback" messages may be omitted.

About security

To prevent spam in the inquiry form, this Site uses Google Forms.
  • Identity verification via Google account (optional)
  • Submission limit (one submission per user)
  • Google's standard reCAPTCHA / bot protection

Please note

  • We cannot accept gameplay strategy or battle advice inquiries
  • For inquiries about official Pokémon products, please contact the official site
  • Spam, sales pitches, and unrelated inquiries will not be answered
  • This Site does not publish an email address. Contact is only accepted via the form above

Before contacting us, please also review the Terms, Privacy Policy, and Disclaimer.

How to Use

How to use each tool and make the most of the site

What is PchamDB?

PchamDB is an unofficial fan database for Nintendo's competitive game Pokémon Champions. It brings together everything you need to prepare for battle—base stats, types, abilities, moves, type matchups, and damage calculation—all in one place, so you can build, look up, and confirm your team on a single site without jumping between page after page of strategy guides. Every feature is free to use right in your browser, with no sign-up or installation required. The site is also available in 9 languages, so Pokémon Champions fans all over the world—starting with Japanese and English speakers—can make the most of it.

How to Use Each Tool

  • Pokémon DB is a Pokédex-style tool for searching and browsing every Pokémon's base stats, types, abilities, and learnable moves in one list. Type a name or ability into the search box to filter, and click a stat column to sort by value. Click a name to open its details and move list—handy for finding the centerpiece of your team. Open · Detailed guide
  • Team Builder lets you register your six Pokémon and automatically visualizes your whole team's type matchups, weaknesses, and offensive coverage. Gaps in your typing become obvious at a glance, and once you set natures and EVs it can calculate actual stats too. Save multiple teams as tabs to compare them side by side—ideal for finding the right partner while you fine-tune a build. Open · Detailed guide
  • Move List lets you search every move by power, accuracy, PP, secondary effect, and priority, along with which Pokémon can learn it. You can sort by column header and filter by conditions such as priority moves or contact moves, making it easy to pick candidates for an open moveslot or refine a set. Open
  • Type Chart is a matrix showing the offensive and defensive matchups across all 18 types. You can view it from both the attacking and defending side, and sorting by fewest weaknesses instantly reveals which types are sturdiest on defense. It's also handy for seeing which moves hit super effectively and for working out dual-type matchups. Open
  • Battle Simulator calculates damage based on actual stats, held items, and nature modifiers. It handles guaranteed KO ranges (how many hits it takes to faint a target), Focus Sash activation, and held-item multipliers. You can confirm bulk-investment thresholds and the outcome of a trade in hard numbers before you ever battle. Open

Who It's For

It's ideal for competitive players who are serious about building teams for ranked matches and tournaments. It helps when you want to patch up matchup gaps, lock in a move set, or verify bulk thresholds with real numbers. It also works as an entry point for newcomers to learn how to read type matchups and base stats.

About the Data and Updates

The data shown here has been compiled independently and is revised as the game is updated and new information comes to light. We strive for accuracy, but updates may lag behind or errors may remain, so please double-check the in-game display when making final decisions. This is a personally run unofficial fan site with no affiliation whatsoever with Nintendo, The Pokémon Company, or any related companies. If you spot a mistake or have a request, please reach out via Contact.

FAQ

Is PchamDB free to use?
Yes. All features are free to use with no sign-up required.

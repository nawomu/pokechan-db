Site mascot Pcham is cheering on your battle prep!

PchamDB is an unofficial fan database
that helps with battle prep and team building for Pokémon Champions.

Note: The data tools (Pokédex / Party Checker / Move List) are currently available in Japanese only. An English-language data layer is in progress (see Making-of). All Pokémon names, moves, abilities, and types in the underlying database will use official localization data from PokeAPI.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

PchamDB (the "Site") respects the privacy of its users and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled on the Site.

Note: This English version is provided for convenience. In case of any discrepancy, the Japanese original shall prevail.

1. Information Collected

The Site may collect the following information:

1-1. Access Logs

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system type
  • Referrer (origin of access)
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages viewed

This information is used solely for improving Site operations and statistical analysis.

1-2. Cookies and LocalStorage

The Site uses cookies and LocalStorage to enhance the user experience.

  • LocalStorage: Stores checklist feature state (e.g., C1/C2/C3) within the user's browser only. It is never sent to the server.
  • Cookies: May be used for access analysis and ad delivery (details below).

Cookies can be disabled in your browser settings. Even with cookies disabled, basic functions of the Site remain available, although some features may be limited.

1-3. Collection of Personal Information

Except for cases where users contact us through the inquiry form, the Site does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as names or email addresses.

2. Analytics Tools

The Site uses analytics tools to understand usage patterns.

The Site uses "Google Analytics," provided by Google LLC. This tool uses cookies to collect traffic data. The traffic data is collected anonymously and does not identify individuals.

To disable Google Analytics, you can install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

2-2. Cloudflare Analytics

The Site may use "Cloudflare Web Analytics," provided by Cloudflare, Inc. This service does not use cookies and gathers access statistics in a privacy-respecting manner.
